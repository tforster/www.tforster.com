ABOUT ME

Creative Technology Director, idea factory, tinkerer, cyclist, hiker, vegetarian living in the wilds of downtown Toronto

  • Vegetarian since I was 20 (and no, I have never missed meat)

  • Love cooking and trying new foods. Indian is still one of my all-time favourites.
